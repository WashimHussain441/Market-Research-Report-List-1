<p><strong>The Opacimeter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Filter Paper Opacimeter</li><li>Transmissive Opacimeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The opacimeter market is primarily segmented into two types: filter paper opacimeters and transmissive opacimeters. Filter paper opacimeters measure the opacity of materials by assessing light transmission through filter paper, making them suitable for various industrial applications. Transmissive opacimeters, on the other hand, gauge opacity by analyzing light transmission through a sample in a controlled environment, providing precise measurements. Both types cater to industries such as packaging, paper manufacturing, and printing, where opacity is a critical quality parameter.</p></p>
