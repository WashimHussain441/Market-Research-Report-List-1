<p><p>The surface mounting adhesives market is divided into two main types: non-conductive adhesives and conductive adhesives. Non-conductive adhesives are primarily used for securing electronic components without affecting electrical performance, offering high insulation properties and reliability. In contrast, conductive adhesives facilitate electrical connectivity, commonly utilized in applications where traditional soldering is impractical. Both types are critical in electronics assembly, enhancing the performance and durability of devices while catering to specific application needs in various industries.</p></p>
