<p><ul><li>Near Infrared</li><li>Medium Infrared</li><li>Far Infrared</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared heat lamp market is categorized into three types based on wavelength: near infrared, medium infrared, and far infrared. Near infrared lamps emit shorter wavelengths, offering quick heating and deep tissue penetration, making them ideal for therapeutic uses. Medium infrared lamps provide balanced warmth, suitable for heating larger areas. Far infrared lamps utilize longer wavelengths, producing gentle heat that is absorbed by the body, often used in saunas and therapeutic applications for relaxation and detoxification.</p></p>
