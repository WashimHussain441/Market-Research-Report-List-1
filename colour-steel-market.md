<p><ul><li>PE Coated Steel</li><li>HDP Coated Steel</li><li>SMP Coated Steel</li><li>PVDF Coated Steel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The color steel market comprises various coated steel types, each suited for specific applications. PE coated steel features a polyethylene finish, offering good weather resistance and color retention, ideal for interior and exterior use. HDP coated steel utilizes high-durability polyester for enhanced UV and chemical resistance, suitable for harsh environments. SMP coated steel employs a modified polyester finish, providing a balance of durability and cost-effectiveness. PVDF coated steel has a fluoropolymer coating, delivering exceptional longevity and resistance to extreme weather, making it perfect for premium applications.</p></p>
