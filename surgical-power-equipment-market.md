<p><p>The Surgical Power Equipment market is categorized into three main types: electric-powered, battery-operated, and pneumatic-powered tools. Electric-powered devices are known for their efficiency and consistent power supply, making them ideal for lengthy surgeries. Battery-operated tools offer mobility and convenience, allowing surgical teams to operate without reliance on stationary power sources. Pneumatic-powered equipment utilizes compressed air, providing robust performance while minimizing heat generation during procedures. Each type caters to different surgical environments and requirements, enhancing operational flexibility and precision.</p></p>
