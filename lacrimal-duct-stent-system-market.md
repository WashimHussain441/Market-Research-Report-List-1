<p><p>The Lacrimal Duct Stent System market features two primary types: Mono Canalicular Stents and Canalicular Stents. Mono Canalicular Stents are designed to support a single canaliculus, facilitating tear drainage from the eye; they are often used in cases of partial obstruction. Canalicular Stents, on the other hand, target both canaliculi, providing more comprehensive drainage solutions for severe blockages or conditions affecting both ducts. Each type is essential for enhancing patient comfort and maintaining ocular health through effective tear management.</p></p>
