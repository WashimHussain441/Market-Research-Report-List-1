<p><strong>The PET Sheet Extrusion Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Twin Screw Extruder</li><li>Single Screw Extruder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PET sheet extrusion equipment market primarily features two types of extruders: twin screw extruders and single screw extruders. Twin screw extruders consist of two intermeshing screws that enhance mixing and material processing, resulting in higher efficiency and better-quality sheets, suitable for complex formulations. In contrast, single screw extruders utilize a single screw for material transport and melting, providing simplicity and cost-effectiveness, making them ideal for standard applications. Both types cater to different production needs in the PET sheet industry.</p></p>
