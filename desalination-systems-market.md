<p><p>The desalination systems market comprises three primary types: thermal technology, membrane technology, and others. Thermal technology uses heat to evaporate water, then condenses the vapor to separate salt, making it effective in regions with abundant energy sources. Membrane technology, primarily reverse osmosis, forces water through semi-permeable membranes to remove salts and impurities, favored for its efficiency and lower energy consumption. The "others" category includes emerging methods like solar desalination and electrochemical processes, diversifying the market's technological landscape.</p></p>
