<p><p>The enzymes for food processing market can be categorized into six main types: </p><p>1. **Oxidoreductases** facilitate oxidation-reduction reactions, enhancing flavor and color.</p><p>2. **Transferases** transfer functional groups, improving texture and stability.</p><p>3. **Hydrolases** break down complex molecules through hydrolysis, aiding in digestion and preservation.</p><p>4. **Isomerases** convert molecules into their isomers, optimizing flavor profiles.</p><p>5. **Lyases** add or remove chemical groups to form double bonds, enhancing food structure.</p><p>6. **Ligases** join two molecules, improving product consistency and quality.</p></p>
