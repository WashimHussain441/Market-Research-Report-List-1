<p><p>The Readout Integrated Circuits (ROIC) market is primarily categorized into two types: Digital Readout Integrated Circuits and Analog Readout Integrated Circuits. Digital ROICs convert analog signals into digital data, enabling high precision and easier integration with digital systems, making them ideal for modern applications. In contrast, Analog ROICs maintain continuous signal formats, offering real-time processing with minimal latency, suitable for traditional analog systems and applications requiring direct signal manipulation. Both types play crucial roles in various electronic applications and industries.</p></p>
