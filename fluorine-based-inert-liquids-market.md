<p><p>The fluorine-based inert liquids market is primarily divided into two categories: per- and polyfluoroalkyl substances (PFAS) and perfluoroether (PFPE) compounds. PFAS are synthetic chemicals known for their water and oil repellency, making them useful in various applications, including firefighting foams and industrial processes. PFPEs, on the other hand, are specialized lubricants and solvents with excellent thermal stability and low toxicity. Both types are crucial in industries like electronics, aerospace, and automotive for their exceptional chemical resistance and performance.</p></p>
