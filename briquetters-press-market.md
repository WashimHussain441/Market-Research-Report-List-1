<p><p>The briquetting press market is segmented into three main types: Screw Briquette Press, Hydraulic Briquette Press, and Punching Briquette Press. The Screw Briquette Press utilizes a screw mechanism to compress biomass into briquettes, featuring continuous operation and efficient energy utilization. The Hydraulic Briquette Press employs hydraulic pressure for high-density briquettes, ideal for various materials. The Punching Briquette Press, on the other hand, uses a punch and die system to form briquettes, often allowing for diverse shapes and sizes. Each type meets specific industrial needs and processes.</p></p>
