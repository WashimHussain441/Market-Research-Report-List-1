<p><ul><li>Whole Pizza Boxes</li><li>Pizza Slice Boxes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pizza box market is primarily divided into two types: whole pizza boxes and pizza slice boxes. Whole pizza boxes are designed to accommodate an entire pizza, ensuring proper support and insulation for delivery and takeout. They are typically made from sturdier cardboard to maintain temperature and prevent grease leakage. In contrast, pizza slice boxes are meant for individual slices, providing a convenient option for consumers. These are often smaller and lightweight, making them ideal for quick service and portion control.</p></p>
