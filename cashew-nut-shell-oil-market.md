<p><p>The cashew nut shell oil market is primarily categorized into two types: Raw Cashew Nut Shell Liquid (CNSL) and Technical Cashew Nut Shell Liquid (TCNSL). Raw CNSL is derived directly from the cashew nut shell, retaining most of its natural properties and is used in industries such as varnishes, adhesives, and oil production. TCNSL, on the other hand, undergoes refinement to enhance its purity and quality, making it suitable for more specialized applications in pharmaceuticals, rubber, and surfactants.</p></p>
