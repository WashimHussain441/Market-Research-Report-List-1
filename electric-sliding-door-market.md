<p><ul><li>Top-mounted Type</li><li>Ground Rail Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Sliding Door market is categorized into two main types: Top-mounted and Ground Rail. </p><p>Top-mounted types feature a track system installed above the door, allowing smooth operation without ground obstructions, making them ideal for interior spaces with limited floor interference. In contrast, Ground Rail types install tracks directly on the floor, ensuring stability and reliable movement. These are well-suited for larger openings and high-traffic areas, enhancing accessibility while accommodating various design preferences and architectural styles.</p></p>
