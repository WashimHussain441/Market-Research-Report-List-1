<p><p>The oil-water separation purifier market includes two main types: hydraulic and sealed. Hydraulic type purifiers rely on gravity and pressure for separation, effectively managing larger volumes of oil and water. They are often used in industrial applications. In contrast, sealed type purifiers employ a closed system to prevent contamination and environmental exposure, making them suitable for facilities requiring higher safety standards. Both types focus on environmental protection and reclaiming oil from wastewater, catering to different operational needs.</p></p>
