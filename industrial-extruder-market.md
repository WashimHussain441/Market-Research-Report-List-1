<p><strong>The Industrial Extruder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Screw</li><li>Twin Screw</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial extruder market is primarily divided into two types: single screw and twin screw extruders. Single screw extruders are characterized by a single helical screw that facilitates material processing and shaping, suitable for applications like thermoplastics. In contrast, twin screw extruders employ two intermeshing screws, allowing for enhanced mixing and distribution of materials, making them ideal for complex formulations and higher viscosity materials. The choice between them depends on specific processing requirements and desired product properties.</p></p>
