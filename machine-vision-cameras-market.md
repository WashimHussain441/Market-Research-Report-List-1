<p><strong>The Machine Vision Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD Camera</li><li>CMOS Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The machine vision camera market primarily consists of CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) cameras. CCD cameras are known for their high image quality and low noise, making them ideal for applications requiring precision, such as industrial inspections. Conversely, CMOS cameras offer higher speeds, lower power consumption, and the ability to integrate additional features on a single chip, making them versatile for real-time processing tasks. Both types cater to diverse industries, including manufacturing, robotics, and quality control.</p></p>
