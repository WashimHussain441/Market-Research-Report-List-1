<p><p>Packaging testing services encompass three main types: physical, chemical, and microbiological testing. Physical testing evaluates the durability, strength, and performance of packaging materials under various conditions, ensuring they protect the product effectively. Chemical testing assesses the interaction between the packaging and its contents, identifying any potential chemical reactions that could compromise product safety. Microbiological testing focuses on detecting harmful microorganisms, ensuring that packaging maintains product integrity and safety throughout its shelf life. Together, these tests help ensure quality and compliance.</p></p>
