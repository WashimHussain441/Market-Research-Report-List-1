<p><p>The zeolite for detergents market primarily features four types: Zeolite A, Zeolite P, Zeolite X, and Zeolite AX. Zeolite A is widely used for its effective calcium and magnesium ion exchange properties, enhancing detergent performance. Zeolite P serves similarly but is more effective in high-temperature applications. Zeolite X offers superior adsorption capabilities for adjuvants and fragrances, while Zeolite AX combines features of both Zeolite A and X, catering to diverse formulations. Each type optimizes cleaning efficiency in laundry and dishwashing products.</p></p>
