<p><strong>The Digital Assistant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cloud-Based</li><li>On-Premise</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital assistants are categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based digital assistants operate via remote servers, enabling scalability and easy updates while offering accessibility from various devices. They typically require a subscription model. In contrast, on-premise digital assistants are installed locally on a company's infrastructure, providing enhanced control over data security and customization. While on-premise solutions may have higher upfront costs, they can be preferable for organizations with strict compliance requirements.</p></p>
