<p><p>The standard treadmill market is divided into two primary types: manual treadmills and motorized treadmills. Manual treadmills require users to generate their movement through walking or running on a belt, offering a simple, low-tech option with no electricity needed. In contrast, motorized treadmills feature an electric motor that drives the belt, providing a consistent speed and various settings for incline and workout programs. These motorized options generally appeal to a broader audience for their convenience and advanced features, catering to diverse fitness levels.</p></p>
