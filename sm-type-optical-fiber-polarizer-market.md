<p><p>The SM Type Optical Fiber Polarizer market is categorized into two primary types: Embedded Type and Ordinary Type. Embedded Type polarizers are integrated within the fiber structure, enhancing performance by minimizing optical losses and offering better versatility in applications. Ordinary Type polarizers, on the other hand, are external optical components that can be used with various fibers, providing flexibility but potentially at the cost of efficiency. Both types serve critical roles in applications requiring precise polarization control in optical systems.</p></p>
