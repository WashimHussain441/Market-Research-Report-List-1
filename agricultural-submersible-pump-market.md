<p><ul><li>Oil-Immersed Type</li><li>Water Immersion Type</li><li>Dry Type</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The agricultural submersible pump market comprises several types based on design and operation. Oil-immersed pumps use oil for cooling and lubrication, ensuring efficient operation in deep water. Water immersion pumps operate fully submerged, utilizing the surrounding water for cooling, making them ideal for irrigation. Dry type pumps, installed above ground, are not submerged and are typically easier to maintain. Other market types may include specialized pumps designed for unique agricultural needs, supporting diverse farming applications.</p></p>
