Due to its eco-friendly nature and biodegradability, NCC is increasingly used in various industries, including pharmaceuticals, food, cosmetics, and packaging.</p><p>The Nano Crystal Cellulose Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.4% during the forecast period.
