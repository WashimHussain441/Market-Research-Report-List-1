<p><ul><li>Linear</li><li>Non-linear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IC Power Controller market is categorized into Linear and Non-linear types based on power regulation methods. Linear controllers adjust voltage or current through continuous, smooth variations, offering precise control with minimal noise, ideal for sensitive applications. Non-linear controllers, like switching regulators, rapidly switch power on and off, achieving high efficiency and compact designs, suitable for high-power systems. Both types cater to diverse needs, balancing precision, efficiency, and application-specific requirements in electronics and industrial systems.</p></p>
