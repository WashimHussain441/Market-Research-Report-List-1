<p><p>Waste-to-energy systems convert waste into usable energy, primarily through several methods: </p><p>1. **Incineration** burns waste at high temperatures, producing electricity and reducing waste volume. </p><p>2. **Gasification** involves converting organic material into synthetic gas, which can be used for power generation or chemical production. </p><p>3. **Pyrolysis** thermally decomposes waste in the absence of oxygen, yielding bio-oil, gas, and char. </p><p>4. **Others** include anaerobic digestion and plasma arc technologies, each offering unique processes for energy recovery from waste.</p></p>
