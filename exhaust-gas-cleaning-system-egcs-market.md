<p><strong>The Exhaust Gas Cleaning System (EGCS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Loop Exhaust Gas Purification System</li><li>Closed Loop Exhaust Gas Purification System</li><li>Mixed Waste Gas Purification System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>An Exhaust Gas Cleaning System (EGCS) is designed to reduce sulfur oxide emissions from ships and industrial sources. The market consists of three main types: </p><p>1. **Open Loop EGCS** utilizes seawater for scrubbing, discharging treated water back into the ocean, suitable for certain maritime conditions. </p><p>2. **Closed Loop EGCS** recycles water, adding fresh water and chemicals, ideal for areas with strict discharge regulations. </p><p>3. **Mixed Waste Gas Purification System** combines elements of both, offering flexibility in various operational environments.</p></p>
