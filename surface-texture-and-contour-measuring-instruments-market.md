<p><p>The Surface Texture and Contour Measuring Instruments market is divided into two main types: contact and non-contact measuring instruments. Contact surface texture instruments utilize tactile methods, where a probe physically contacts the surface to gather data about its texture and contour. In contrast, non-contact instruments employ optical or laser technology to assess surface characteristics without physical contact, allowing for higher speed and reduced risk of damage to sensitive surfaces. Both types are essential for quality control in various industries.</p></p>
