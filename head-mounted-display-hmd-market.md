<p><p>Head Mounted Displays (HMDs) are devices worn on the head, providing immersive visual experiences. The market is categorized into three types: </p><p>1. Slide-on HMDs, which attach to existing glasses, enhancing visuals without removing prescription eyewear.</p><p>2. Discrete HMDs, resembling traditional eyewear but designed solely for virtual experiences, offering portability and style.</p><p>3. Integrated HMDs, which combine display, processing, and sensors into a single unit, delivering fully immersive experiences for gaming and professional applications. Each type caters to different user needs and preferences.</p></p>
