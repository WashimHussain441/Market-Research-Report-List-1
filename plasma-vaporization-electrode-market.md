<p><strong>The Plasma Vaporization Electrode Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bipolar</li><li>Monopolar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plasma vaporization electrode market is segmented into bipolar and monopolar types based on their operational mechanisms. Bipolar electrodes utilize both electrodes in the same device for efficient energy transfer, enhancing tissue interaction and minimizing collateral damage. In contrast, monopolar electrodes employ a single active electrode, grounding through a separate return, which allows for more versatility in surgical applications but may lead to increased thermal spread. Each type caters to specific needs in minimally invasive procedures and technological innovation.</p></p>
