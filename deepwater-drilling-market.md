Floating platforms, including semi-submersibles and tension-leg platforms, are designed for deeper waters, offering flexibility and mobility. Other market types encompass subsea systems and drilling ships, which utilize advanced technology for deepwater exploration, enabling efficient resource extraction in challenging marine environments.</p></p>
