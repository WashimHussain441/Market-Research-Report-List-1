<p><ul><li>Primers</li><li>Probes</li><li>Intermediate-scale Synthesis Oligos</li><li>Large-scale Synthesis Oligos</li><li>Linkers and Adaptors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The DNA oligonucleotide synthesis market is categorized into several types based on application and scale. Primers are short sequences used in PCR and sequencing. Probes are designed for specific target detection in assays. Intermediate-scale synthesis oligos cater to research needs, while large-scale synthesis oligos support commercial production and high-throughput applications. Lastly, linkers and adaptors facilitate the connection between different DNA fragments, essential for cloning and assembly processes. Each type plays a critical role in genomic research and biotechnology.</p></p>
