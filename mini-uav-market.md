<p><ul><li>Fixed Wing</li><li>Rotor Wing</li><li>Parasol Wing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mini UAV market comprises three primary types: Fixed Wing, Rotor Wing, and Parasol Wing. Fixed Wing UAVs are designed for long endurance and cover large areas, making them ideal for surveillance and mapping. Rotor Wing UAVs, or multirotors, offer vertical takeoff and landing capabilities, excelling in agility and maneuverability for close-range tasks. Parasol Wing UAVs combine features of both, featuring a hybrid design that allows for extended flight times while maintaining versatility in different operational environments.</p></p>
