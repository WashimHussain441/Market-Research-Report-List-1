<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Dual Channels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The TENS (Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulation) machine market is segmented into single-channel and dual-channel types. Single-channel units deliver electrical impulses through one lead, suitable for localized pain relief, making them portable and cost-effective. In contrast, dual-channel machines have two leads, allowing simultaneous treatment of multiple areas. This versatility enhances pain management efficiency, catering to patients with broader pain coverage needs. Both types are increasingly popular in home healthcare and rehabilitation settings, driven by the growing demand for non-invasive pain relief solutions.</p></p>
