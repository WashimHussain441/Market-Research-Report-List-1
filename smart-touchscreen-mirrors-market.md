<p><p>Smart touchscreen mirrors incorporate various display technologies, including TFT (Thin-Film Transistor), TN (Twisted Nematic), and LCD (Liquid Crystal Display). TFT displays offer superior color reproduction and viewing angles, making them popular for high-quality interfaces. TN panels, while cheaper, provide faster response times but limit viewing angles and color quality. LCD technology serves as the foundation for both TFT and TN, utilizing liquid crystals for image display. Each type caters to different performance needs, price points, and application contexts in smart mirror devices.</p></p>
