<p><p>The residential humidifier market includes various types, primarily vapor type humidifiers and water spray humidifiers. Vapor type humidifiers generate steam by boiling water, releasing warm moisture into the air, which can help alleviate dry conditions. In contrast, water spray humidifiers utilize a fine mist of water to increase humidity levels, often operating at cooler temperatures. Both types aim to improve indoor air quality, enhance comfort, and support respiratory health by maintaining optimal humidity levels within homes.</p></p>
