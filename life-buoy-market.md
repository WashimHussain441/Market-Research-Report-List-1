<p><p>Life buoy market types, such as horseshoe and ring buoys, serve distinct safety needs in water-based environments. </p><p>Horseshoe buoys are semi-circular, designed for easy grip and rescue, often used in bays and marinas. Their shape allows for quick access and deployment.</p><p>Ring buoys, on the other hand, are circular and provide buoyancy for a person in distress. They are typically used in swimming pools and along coastlines, allowing for immediate throwing to an individual in need of assistance.</p></p>
