<p><ul><li>Braking Torque Control</li><li>Engine Torque Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>A Traction Control System (TCS) enhances vehicle stability by preventing wheel spin during acceleration. It utilizes two primary market types: Braking Torque Control and Engine Torque Control. Braking Torque Control applies brakes to individual wheels when slip is detected, ensuring optimal traction. Conversely, Engine Torque Control reduces engine power by adjusting fuel delivery or timing, helping maintain grip without losing momentum. Together, these systems improve vehicle safety and performance, particularly in challenging driving conditions.</p></p>
