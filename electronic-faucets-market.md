<p><p>The electronic faucets market is primarily divided into two types: touchless and touched electronic faucets. Touchless electronic faucets operate using sensors, allowing users to activate water flow without physical contact, promoting hygiene and convenience. They are commonly used in public restrooms and kitchens. In contrast, touched electronic faucets require users to press a lever or button to dispense water. These faucets provide a user-friendly experience, often featuring advanced technology for precise temperature control and water conservation, making them ideal for residential and commercial applications.</p></p>
