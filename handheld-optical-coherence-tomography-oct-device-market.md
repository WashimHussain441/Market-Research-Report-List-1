<p><strong>The Handheld Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spectral-Domain OCT</li><li>Swept-Source OCT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Handheld Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) device market is primarily categorized into two types: Spectral-Domain OCT (SD-OCT) and Swept-Source OCT (SS-OCT). SD-OCT uses a broad-spectrum light source and relies on interferometry to capture high-resolution images of tissue, making it suitable for detailed assessment in various applications. SS-OCT, on the other hand, employs a tunable laser source that sweeps across wavelengths, allowing for faster imaging and deeper tissue penetration, ideal for advanced diagnostic uses in ophthalmology and other medical fields.</p></p>
