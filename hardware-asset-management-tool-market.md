<p><p>Hardware Asset Management (HAM) tools can be categorized into two main market types: Cloud-Based and On-Premises. Cloud-Based HAM solutions are hosted on the vendor's servers and accessed via the internet, offering scalability, lower upfront costs, and ease of updates. Conversely, On-Premises HAM tools are installed directly on a company's servers, providing greater control and customization but requiring higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance. Organizations choose between these options based on their operational needs, budget, and IT infrastructure.</p></p>
