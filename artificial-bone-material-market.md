<p><p>The artificial bone material market is categorized into three types: inorganic, organic, and composite materials. Inorganic materials, such as calcium phosphate, mimic bone mineral composition, promoting effective integration with natural bone. Organic materials, like collagen or gelatin, support cellular growth and enhance biocompatibility. Composite materials combine both inorganic and organic components to leverage the strengths of each, providing improved mechanical properties and biological performance. Together, these types cater to diverse orthopedic applications and advancements in regenerative medicine.</p></p>
