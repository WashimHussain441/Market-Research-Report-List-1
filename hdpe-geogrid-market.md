<p><p>The HDPE geogrid market is categorized into two main types: biaxial tension and uniaxial tension. Biaxial tensile geogrids are designed to handle loads in both horizontal and vertical directions, making them suitable for a wide range of applications, such as soil reinforcement and stabilization in various construction projects. Conversely, uniaxial tensile geogrids are engineered to handle significant loads in a single direction, primarily used in applications like retaining walls and slope stabilization, where strength alignment is critical.</p></p>
