<p><p>High Pressure Sodium (HPS) lamps are a type of gas-discharge lamp that produces light by creating an arc between sodium and other gases under high pressure. This growth can be attributed to increasing urbanization, infrastructure development, and the rising need for efficient lighting solutions. Additionally, the growing awareness of energy conservation and advancements in lighting technology are further driving demand.</p><p>Recent trends in the market indicate a shift towards energy-efficient lighting solutions, with many regions transitioning from traditional HPS lamps to LED alternatives. However, HPS lamps remain popular in specific applications such as street lighting and agriculture, owing to their superior light output and plant growth capabilities. Moreover, government initiatives supporting the adoption of efficient lighting technologies are expected to bolster market expansion, ensuring that HPS lamps continue to play a vital role in the global lighting landscape.</p></p>
