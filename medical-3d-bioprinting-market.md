<p><p>The Medical 3D Bioprinting market encompasses various technologies, including Stereolithography (SLA), Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM), and Laminated Object Manufacturing (LOM), among others. SLA uses UV light to cure photopolymer resins layer by layer, creating intricate structures. FDM involves extruding thermoplastic materials to build prototypes and biological models. LOM layers adhesive-coated materials and cuts them to form 3D objects. Each method offers unique advantages for applications like tissue engineering, organ modeling, and regenerative medicine, driving innovation in healthcare solutions.</p></p>
