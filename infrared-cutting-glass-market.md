<p><strong>The Infrared Cutting Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorption Type</li><li>Reflection Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared cutting glass market comprises two main types: absorption type and reflection type. Absorption type glass utilizes specific materials that absorb infrared radiation, converting it to heat, which enhances cutting efficiency. In contrast, reflection type glass reflects infrared radiation, preventing heat buildup and protecting underlying materials. Both types serve various industrial applications, with absorption type often favored for high-precision cutting and reflection type used in situations requiring heat management and surface protection during the cutting process.</p></p>
