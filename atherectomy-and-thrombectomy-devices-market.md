<p><strong>The Atherectomy and Thrombectomy Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rheolytic</li><li>Aspiration</li><li>Rotational</li><li>Ultrasound Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Atherectomy and thrombectomy devices are crucial in treating vascular conditions by removing plaque or thrombus. Rheolytic devices utilize a high-velocity saline jet to break down clots, making extraction easier. Aspiration devices use suction to remove thrombus directly from the site. Rotational devices employ a rotating blade to shave off plaque or clot material, while ultrasound devices use high-frequency sound waves to fragment thrombus, enabling easier removal. Each device type offers unique mechanisms to enhance procedure efficacy and patient outcomes.</p></p>
