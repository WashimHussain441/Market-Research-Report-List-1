<p><p>The renal cyst treatment market comprises three main types: sclerotherapy, surgery, and others. Sclerotherapy involves injecting a sclerosing agent into the cyst, causing it to shrink. Surgery may include laparoscopic techniques to remove larger or symptomatic cysts, providing immediate relief. The "others" category encompasses alternative treatments, such as percutaneous drainage and observation for asymptomatic cysts. Each method is tailored to the cyst's size, symptoms, and patient health, aiming to effectively manage renal cysts with minimal complications.</p></p>
