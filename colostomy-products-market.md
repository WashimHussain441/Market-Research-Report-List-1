<p><ul><li>One Piece Bag</li><li>Two Piece Bag</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The colostomy products market is primarily divided into two categories: one-piece bags and two-piece bags. One-piece bags integrate the adhesive and pouch into a single unit, simplifying application and disposal. They are user-friendly and ideal for active individuals. In contrast, two-piece bags consist of a separate adhesive skin barrier that attaches to the pouch, allowing for ease of changing the pouch alone without disturbing the adhesive. This offers flexibility and customization for users' comfort and needs.</p></p>
