<p><ul><li>Deep Filter</li><li>Membrane Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Filtration Sterilization Equipment market encompasses various types, notably deep filters and membrane filters. Deep filters utilize a thick porous medium to capture particles and microbes, effectively providing depth filtration. Conversely, membrane filters consist of thin sheets that allow the passage of fluids while blocking larger contaminants, offering precise filtration. Both types are essential in industries such as pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and biotechnology, ensuring product safety and compliance with stringent sterilization standards.</p></p>
