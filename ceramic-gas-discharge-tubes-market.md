<p><p>The Ceramic Gas Discharge Tubes market is categorized into two main types: Surface Mount Gas Tube Arresters and Through Hole Gas Discharge Tubes. Surface Mount Gas Tube Arresters are designed for direct mounting onto the surface of a circuit board, providing compact protection against voltage surges. In contrast, Through Hole Gas Discharge Tubes are inserted through holes in the circuit board, offering robust protection in larger, more traditional electronic designs. Both types effectively safeguard electronic components from transient overvoltage conditions.</p></p>
