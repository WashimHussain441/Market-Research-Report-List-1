<p><p>The magnetic level gauge switch market includes several types of switches: </p><p>1. **Single Pole Double Throw (SPDT) Switch**: This switch allows the user to control one load with two different circuits, enabling versatile level measurement and control.</p><p>2. **Double Pole Double Throw (DPDT) Switch**: This switch manages two separate circuits, offering more complex control and monitoring options for level gauges in various applications.</p><p>3. **Pneumatic Switch**: Utilizing air pressure, this switch provides a reliable, remote-trigger option for level gauging, ideal for environments where electrical options are impractical or unsafe.</p></p>
