<p><strong>The Optical Encoder Ics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transmissive Type</li><li>Reflective Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical encoder integrated circuits (ICs) market is primarily categorized into two types: transmissive and reflective. Transmissive type encoders utilize a light source and photodetector, sensing light passing through a rotating disk with patterned slots. They offer high precision in applications like robotics and automation. Reflective type encoders, on the other hand, use a light source and a reflective surface, detecting light reflected back to the sensor. This type is often preferred for its simplicity and cost-effectiveness in less demanding applications.</p></p>
