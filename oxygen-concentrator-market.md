<p><p>An oxygen concentrator is a medical device that delivers concentrated oxygen to individuals experiencing respiratory issues or conditions that result in low oxygen levels. It functions by drawing in ambient air, filtering out nitrogen, and delivering a higher concentration of oxygen to the user through a nasal cannula or mask. These devices are essential in both hospital settings and home-care environments, especially for patients with chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD), pneumonia, and other respiratory ailments.</p><p>The Oxygen Concentrator Market is anticipated to grow significantly, with an expected compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 8.4% during the forecast period.
