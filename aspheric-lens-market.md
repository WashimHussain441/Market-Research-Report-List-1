<p><p>An aspheric lens is a type of optical lens with a non-spherical surface profile, designed to reduce spherical aberration and improve image quality. Unlike traditional spherical lenses, which have a uniform curvature, aspheric lenses feature a more complex geometry that allows for better light focusing, reduced distortion, and enhanced performance in various applications. These lenses are widely used in industries such as consumer electronics, automotive, healthcare, and aerospace, where precision optics are critical.</p><p>The Aspheric Lens Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for high-quality imaging systems and advancements in manufacturing technologies.
