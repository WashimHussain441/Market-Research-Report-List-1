<p><p>Vacuum Gas Oil (VGO) is an intermediate petroleum product derived from the vacuum distillation of crude oil, used primarily in refining processes. The market is divided into two main types: Heavy Vacuum Gas Oil (HVGO) and Light Vacuum Gas Oil (LVGO). HVGO has a higher specific gravity and is richer in aromatic compounds, making it suitable for further processing in hydrocrackers. In contrast, LVGO has a lighter fraction, making it ideal for producing cleaner fuels and lighter products.</p></p>
