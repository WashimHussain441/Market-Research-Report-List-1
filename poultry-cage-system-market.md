<p><p>The poultry cage system market comprises two primary types: semi-automatic and fully automatic systems. Semi-automatic systems require some manual intervention for tasks like feeding and egg collection, offering a balance between labor savings and control. In contrast, fully automatic systems integrate advanced technology for all operations, including feeding, watering, and waste management, minimizing human involvement. Both systems aim to enhance productivity and welfare in poultry farming, with fully automatic systems typically offering greater efficiency and consistency.</p></p>
