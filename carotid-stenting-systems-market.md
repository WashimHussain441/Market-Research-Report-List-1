<p><p>The Carotid Stenting Systems market is divided into three main types: Bare Metal Stents, Polymers, and Others. Bare Metal Stents are made of a metallic alloy providing structural support and patency but may have higher restenosis rates. Polymer stents feature a coating that enhances biocompatibility, potentially reducing complications. The "Others" category includes advanced materials and specialized designs, such as drug-eluting stents that release medication to prevent blockage. Each type addresses specific clinical needs and patient conditions in carotid artery interventions.</p></p>
