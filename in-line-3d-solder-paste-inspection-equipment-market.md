<p><ul><li>Laser-based 3D Solder Paste Inspection Equipment</li><li>PMP-based 3D Solder Paste Inspection Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the line 3D Solder Paste Inspection Equipment market, there are primarily two types: laser-based and PMP-based inspection systems. Laser-based 3D solder paste inspection utilizes lasers to capture the height and volume of solder paste deposits with high precision, ensuring optimal quality control. In contrast, PMP-based (Pattern Matching Process) inspection relies on image processing techniques to analyze the solder paste patterns against predefined templates, offering reliable detection of discrepancies. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing manufacturing accuracy and reducing defects.</p></p>
