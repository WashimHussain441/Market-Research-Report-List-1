<p><p>An Ultrasonic Retro reflective Sensor is a sensing device that utilizes ultrasonic sound waves to detect the presence of objects, measure distances, and facilitate automation processes. These sensors emit sound waves that bounce off nearby objects, allowing them to gauge the distance based on the time it takes for the sound waves to return.
