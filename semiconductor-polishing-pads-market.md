<p><ul><li>Hard CMP Pads</li><li>Soft CMP Pads</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor polishing pads market is divided into two main types: hard CMP pads and soft CMP pads. Hard CMP pads are designed for aggressive chemical-mechanical polishing, typically used for achieving high precision on hard substrate materials. In contrast, soft CMP pads offer enhanced conformability and are better suited for delicate processes, providing a gentler polishing action on softer materials. Each type serves distinct applications in semiconductor manufacturing, catering to the specific requirements of various wafer polishing processes.</p></p>
