<p><p>The Automotive Sledge Testing System market includes two main types: Decelerator Sled Testing Systems and Catapult Sled Testing Systems. Decelerator sleds are designed to simulate vehicle crash scenarios by bringing a vehicle to a rapid stop, enabling the assessment of safety features and occupant protection systems. In contrast, catapult sleds launch vehicles to simulate various impact speeds, allowing for detailed analysis of crash dynamics. Both systems are essential for vehicle design validation and regulatory compliance in automotive safety testing.</p></p>
