<p><p>The on-load tap changing transformer market comprises two primary types: vacuum and oil-immersed transformers. Vacuum-type transformers use a sealed chamber to facilitate tap changing without the need for oil, offering advantages in maintenance and environmental safety. In contrast, oil-immersed transformers utilize insulating oil for cooling and maintaining electrical insulation, which can enhance performance in larger units. Both types serve critical roles in managing voltage levels in power distribution, ensuring reliability and efficiency in electrical networks.</p></p>
