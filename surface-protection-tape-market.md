<p><p>The surface protection tape market comprises various types based on material, notably LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene), PP (Polypropylene), and PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride). LDPE tapes are lightweight, flexible, and resistant to moisture, making them ideal for delicate surfaces. PP tapes are durable and offer excellent tensile strength, suitable for more robust applications. PVC tapes provide superior resistance to chemicals and UV rays, making them effective for outdoor and industrial uses. Each type caters to specific needs in surface protection across diverse industries.</p></p>
