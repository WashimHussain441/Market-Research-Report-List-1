<p><p>The acoustic insulation glass windows market comprises three main types: Hollow Glass, Vacuum Glass, and Laminated Glass. Hollow Glass features an air gap between two sheets, providing reasonable sound isolation. Vacuum Glass, with minimal air between its panes, offers superior acoustic insulation and energy efficiency. Laminated Glass consists of multiple layers bonded together, effectively reducing noise transmission and enhancing safety. Each type caters to varying needs in residential and commercial spaces, focusing on comfort and sound control.</p></p>
