<p><p>Automotive rear-view camera modules enhance driver visibility and safety, primarily using CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) technologies. CCD cameras offer high image quality, excellent low-light performance, and reduced noise, making them ideal for premium vehicles. CMOS cameras, on the other hand, are cost-effective, energy-efficient, and compact, suitable for mass-market applications. Both types are integral to advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS), with CMOS gaining popularity due to its scalability and integration capabilities, while CCD remains preferred for superior image clarity in high-end automotive segments.</p></p>
