<p><ul><li>Vacuum Formed</li><li>Pressure Formed</li><li>Mechanical Formed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermoforming plastic market can be categorized into three main types: vacuum formed, pressure formed, and mechanical formed. Vacuum forming involves heating a thermoplastic sheet and then using a vacuum to shape it over a mold, commonly used for lightweight applications. Pressure forming applies air pressure to enhance detail and thickness, making it suited for complex designs. Mechanical forming utilizes physical forces, such as stretching or drawing, to shape plastics, offering versatility for custom applications in various industries.</p></p>
