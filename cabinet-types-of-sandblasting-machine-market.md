<p><p>The sandblasting machine market is primarily categorized into dry and wet cabinet types. Dry sandblasting machines use compressed air to propel abrasive materials, creating a clean and smooth surface finish. They are ideal for tasks requiring minimal moisture and quick drying. Wet sandblasting machines, on the other hand, combine water with abrasives, reducing dust and health hazards. This method is effective for delicate surfaces and offers a cleaner operation. Each type serves specific applications in industries such as automotive, construction, and metalworking.</p></p>
