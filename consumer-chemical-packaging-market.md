<p><p>The consumer chemical packaging market includes several types, notably barrels, flexitanks, and intermediate bulk containers (IBCs). Barrels are traditionally used for liquid chemicals, offering strong protection and ease of handling. Flexitanks are flexible bladder systems designed to carry bulk liquids in standard containers, maximizing transport efficiency. Intermediate Bulk Containers, or IBCs, are reusable rigid containers ideal for transporting larger volumes of liquids and semi-solids, providing both safety and convenience in logistics. Each type serves specific needs in chemical distribution.</p></p>
