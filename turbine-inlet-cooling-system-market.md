<p><p>The Turbine Inlet Cooling System market comprises three primary types: inlet fogging, chiller systems, and evaporative cooling. Inlet fogging enhances efficiency by injecting moisture into the intake air, lowering its temperature. Chiller systems, typically utilizing refrigerants, provide efficient cooling through heat exchange, ensuring optimal turbine performance. Evaporative cooling relies on water evaporation to cool the incoming air, promoting enhanced turbine output. Each method aims to improve gas turbine efficiency and reliability in power generation applications.</p></p>
