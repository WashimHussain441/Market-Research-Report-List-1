<p><p>The PE-RT (Polyethylene of Raised Temperature Resistance) market includes various types based on the co-monomer used: Ethylene-Octene, Ethylene-Hexene, and Ethylene-Butene. Ethylene-Octene typically offers enhanced flexibility and impact resistance, making it suitable for applications like plumbing and heating systems. Ethylene-Hexene provides improved melt flow and strength, ideal for packaging and industrial applications. Ethylene-Butene delivers high stiffness and good thermal properties, often used in high-temperature applications. Each type caters to specific performance needs and industry requirements.</p></p>
