<p><ul><li>Single Break Isolator</li><li>Double Break Isolator</li><li>Pantograph type Isolator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AC isolator market consists of various types, including the single break isolator, double break isolator, and pantograph type isolator. Single break isolators feature one set of contacts to disconnect the circuit, while double break isolators leverage two sets of contacts for enhanced reliability and safety. Pantograph type isolators are designed for overhead lines, allowing effective disconnection with a flexible design. Each type serves distinct applications in electrical networks, offering varying degrees of reliability and installation requirements.</p></p>
