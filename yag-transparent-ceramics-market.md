<p><p>The YAG (Yttrium Aluminum Garnet) transparent ceramics market features two main types: rare earth doping and undoping. Rare earth doping involves adding specific rare earth elements to enhance properties like luminescence and thermal stability, making the material suitable for applications in laser systems and optoelectronics.
