<p><p>The cylinder pressure regulator market comprises three main types: low pressure regulators, medium pressure regulators, and high pressure regulators. Low pressure regulators are designed to maintain lower outlet pressures, suitable for applications requiring gentle flow control. Medium pressure regulators balance between low and high pressures, offering versatility for various industrial uses. High pressure regulators are built to manage elevated pressure levels, typically found in gas distribution and heavy-duty applications. Each type serves specific needs in the safe and efficient handling of gases.</p></p>
