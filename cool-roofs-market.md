<p><p>The cool roofs market features three primary types: PVC, EPDM, and TPO. PVC roofs are durable, lightweight, and resistant to chemicals and fire, making them ideal for commercial applications. EPDM, a synthetic rubber, offers excellent weather resistance and flexibility, suitable for various roofing structures. TPO combines the best features of rubber and thermoplastics, providing energy efficiency and UV resistance, often used in low-slope roofing projects. Each type contributes to energy savings and improved indoor comfort by reflecting sunlight and reducing heat absorption.</p></p>
