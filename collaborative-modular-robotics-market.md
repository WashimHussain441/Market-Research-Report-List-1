<p><p>The Collaborative Modular Robotics market encompasses various types of robotics designed for flexibility and teamwork in industrial environments. Articulated Modular Robots feature rotating joints for complex movements, while Cartesian Modular Robots operate along straight lines for precise positioning. Selective Compliance Assembly Robot Arms (SCARA) combine lateral flexibility with vertical rigidity to optimize assembly tasks. Parallel Modular Robots utilize multiple arms for enhanced speed and stability, and Collaborative Modular Robots prioritize safe interaction with human workers, promoting efficient co-working in diverse applications.</p></p>
