<p><p>The **Screen Printing Inks Market** includes **Solvent-based Printing Inks** and **Water-based Printing Inks**. Water-based inks use water as a carrier, providing eco-friendliness, softer finishes, and better adhesion to fabrics, making them suitable for textiles and indoor use. Both types cater to diverse industries, with solvent-based excelling in durability and water-based focusing on sustainability and comfort.</p></p>
