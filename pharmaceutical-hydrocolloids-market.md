<p><p>The pharmaceutical hydrocolloids market comprises various types, each with unique properties and applications. Carrageenan and agar, extracted from seaweeds, are primarily employed for their gelling capabilities in drug delivery systems. Xanthan gum, produced through fermentation, acts as a thickening agent, enhancing the viscosity and stability of pharmaceutical products. Each type contributes significantly to formulation efficacy.</p></p>
