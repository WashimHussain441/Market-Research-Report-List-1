<p><p>Through Beam Photoelectric Sensors utilize a light source to detect the presence of an object by breaking an emitted beam. The market comprises two primary types: Laser and LED sensors. Laser sensors emit a focused beam of light, offering high precision and extended range, ideal for long-distance applications. In contrast, LED sensors use visible light, providing a cost-effective solution for shorter detection ranges. Both types are essential in industrial automation, packaging, and safety applications, enhancing operational efficiency.</p></p>
