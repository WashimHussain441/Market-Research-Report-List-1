<p><p>The flat gear motor market is segmented primarily into three types: spur flat gear motors, helical flat gear motors, and others. Spur flat gear motors feature straight teeth and provide high torque with simple design, making them ideal for basic applications. Helical flat gear motors, with angled teeth, offer smoother and quieter operation, suited for higher-speed applications. The "others" category includes specialized gear motors like bevel or planetary types, catering to niche applications requiring distinct performance characteristics.</p></p>
