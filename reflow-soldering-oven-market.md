<p><strong>The Reflow Soldering Oven Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Convection Ovens</li><li>Vapour Phase Ovens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reflow soldering ovens are essential in electronics manufacturing for attaching components to PCBs. There are two main types: convection ovens and vapor phase ovens. Convection ovens use hot air to evenly distribute heat, ensuring reliable soldering of components. They offer flexibility and are widely used due to their efficiency. Vapor phase ovens, on the other hand, utilize a vaporized liquid to create a uniform heating environment, minimizing thermal shock and offering precise temperature control. Both types are critical for high-quality solder joints.</p></p>
