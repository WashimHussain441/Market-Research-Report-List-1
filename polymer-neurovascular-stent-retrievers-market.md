<p><p>The Polymer Neurovascular Stent Retrievers market is characterized by two main types: balloon-expanding stents and self-expanding stents. Balloon-expanding stents are deployed through inflation, allowing for precise placement in the neurovascular system. They provide rigid support and are effective in certain vascular conditions. In contrast, self-expanding stents utilize a material that automatically expands upon deployment, adapting to varying vessel sizes and offering flexibility. Both types play crucial roles in treating neurovascular disorders, enhancing patient outcomes through improved vascular support.</p></p>
