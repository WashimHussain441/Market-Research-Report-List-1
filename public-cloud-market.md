<p><p>The public cloud market encompasses several service models. Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) provides virtualized computing resources over the internet, allowing users to manage hardware and networking. Platform as a Service (PaaS) offers a framework for developers to build, deploy, and manage applications without managing the underlying infrastructure. Software as a Service (SaaS) delivers software applications via the web, eliminating the need for local installation. Business Process as a Service (BPaaS) streamlines business processes through cloud-based solutions, while Cloud Management and Security Services ensure the efficient and secure operation of cloud environments.</p></p>
