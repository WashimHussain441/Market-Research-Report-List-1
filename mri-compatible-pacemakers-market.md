<p><strong>The MRI Compatible Pacemakers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-chamber Pacemakers</li><li>Dual-chamber Pacemakers</li><li>Biventricular/CRT Pacemakers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MRI-compatible pacemaker market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Single-chamber pacemakers**: These devices are designed to stimulate one chamber of the heart, typically the right ventricle, offering a simpler solution for patients with specific pacing needs.</p><p>2. **Dual-chamber pacemakers**: These devices regulate both the atrium and ventricle, enabling more synchronized heartbeats and better overall cardiac function.</p><p>3. **Biventricular/CRT pacemakers**: Used for heart failure patients, these devices improve the heart's efficiency by coordinating contractions in both ventricles, enhancing blood flow and performance.</p></p>
