<p><p>The precancer molecular diagnostics market encompasses various techniques used for early detection of cancer. Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) amplifies specific DNA sequences, enabling the identification of cancer-related mutations. Fluorescence In Situ Hybridization (FISH) detects genetic abnormalities at the chromosomal level using fluorescent probes. DNA sequencing provides a comprehensive analysis of genetic information to reveal mutations associated with cancer. Gene chips, or microarrays, allow simultaneous examination of thousands of genes, facilitating the identification of precancerous markers and enhancing diagnosis.</p></p>
