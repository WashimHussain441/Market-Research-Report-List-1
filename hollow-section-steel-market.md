<p><p>Hollow section steel refers to steel profiles with hollow cross-sections, widely used in construction, infrastructure, and manufacturing. **Rectangular Hollow Section (RHS)** offers high strength and versatility, ideal for structural frameworks. **Square Hollow Section (SHS)** provides uniform strength in all directions, suitable for columns and supports. **Circular Hollow Section (CHS)** excels in load-bearing applications due to its symmetrical shape, often used in pipelines and towers. **Other** types include elliptical or custom profiles, catering to niche engineering and architectural needs, offering flexibility in design and functionality.</p></p>
