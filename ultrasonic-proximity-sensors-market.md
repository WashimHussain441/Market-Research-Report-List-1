<p><p>Ultrasonic proximity sensors are essential in various applications for detecting object presence and distance measurement. The market is primarily divided into two types: Analog and Digital. Analog type sensors provide continuous output proportional to the distance of the detected object, enabling gradual adjustments in applications such as automation and robotics. Conversely, digital type sensors deliver discrete signals, indicating whether an object is within a specific range, making them suitable for simpler applications like object counting and collision detection.</p></p>
