<p><p>Mobile Medical Alert Systems are categorized into two main types: No Cell Phone Needed and Cell Phone Needed. No Cell Phone Needed systems operate independently, using built-in cellular technology to connect users to emergency services without requiring a smartphone or additional devices. Both types ensure timely assistance, enhancing safety and peace of mind for users.</p></p>
