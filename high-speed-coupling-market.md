<p><strong>The High-Speed Coupling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flexible Elastic Coupling</li><li>Torsionally-rigid Flexible Coupling</li><li>Rigid Coupling</li><li>Flange Coupling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-speed coupling market includes various types designed to accommodate different mechanical needs. Flexible elastic couplings absorb misalignments and vibrations, enhancing performance. Torsionally-rigid flexible couplings provide a balance between flexibility and torque transmission, ideal for high-speed applications. Rigid couplings connect shafts securely without flexibility, ensuring precision alignment but less adaptability. Flange couplings join shafts via bolted flanges, facilitating easy assembly and disassembly while maintaining high torque capacity, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications.</p></p>
