<p><p>The LVIT (Linear Variable Inductance Transducer) position sensor market is categorized into DC and AC segments based on the type of excitation current used for operation. DC LVITs use direct current to generate a linear position output, offering advantages such as simplicity and ease of integration in various applications. In contrast, AC LVITs utilize alternating current, often providing higher accuracy and sensitivity. Both types find applications across industries, including automotive, aerospace, and industrial automation, catering to specific measurement needs.</p></p>
