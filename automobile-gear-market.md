<p><ul><li>Skew Shaft Gears</li><li>Intersecting Shaft Gear</li><li>Parallel Shaft Gears</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automobile gear market comprises several types, including skew shaft gears, intersecting shaft gears, and parallel shaft gears. Skew shaft gears facilitate torque transmission between non-parallel shafts, often used in compact designs. Intersecting shaft gears, such as bevel gears, enable power transfer between shafts that intersect at an angle, making them ideal for directional changes. Parallel shaft gears, including spur and helical gears, transmit motion between shafts that run parallel, widely employed in various automotive applications for efficiency and performance.</p></p>
