<p><p>The optical spectroscope market is primarily divided into two types: visible optics and invisible optics. Visible optics focus on the range of light that can be seen by the human eye, making them essential for applications in fields like astronomy and environmental monitoring. Conversely, invisible optics involve wavelengths beyond the visible spectrum, such as infrared and ultraviolet light, and are utilized in various scientific, industrial, and medical applications, including material analysis and healthcare diagnostics. Each type caters to specific analytical needs.</p></p>
