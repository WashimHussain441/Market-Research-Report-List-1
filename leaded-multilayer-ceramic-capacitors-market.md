<p><p>The leaded multilayer ceramic capacitors market features two primary types: radial lead and axial lead. Radial lead type capacitors have leads extending from the same side, allowing for efficient space utilization on printed circuit boards, making them ideal for compact designs. In contrast, axial lead type capacitors have leads on opposite ends, providing a linear arrangement that suits different mounting configurations. Both types are widely used in electronic applications for their reliability, stability, and performance in various environments.</p></p>
