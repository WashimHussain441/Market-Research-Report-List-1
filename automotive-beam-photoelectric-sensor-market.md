<p><strong>The Automotive Beam (Photoelectric) Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through-Beam Photoelectric Sensor</li><li>Reflective Photoelectric Sensor</li><li>Diffuse Photoelectric Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive beam (photoelectric) sensor market includes three main types: Through-Beam, Reflective, and Diffuse photoelectric sensors. Through-Beam sensors consist of separate transmitter and receiver modules, detecting objects by the interruption of emitted light. Reflective sensors combine transmitter and receiver, relying on reflected light for detection, making them suitable for shorter ranges. Diffuse sensors emit light and sense reflections from nearby objects, ideal for detecting surfaces or small items. Each type serves distinct automotive applications, enhancing safety and automation.</p></p>
