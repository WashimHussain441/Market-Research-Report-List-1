<p><p>The **Casted Automotive Components Market** includes various manufacturing techniques like **Squeeze Casting** and **Investment Casting**. Squeeze casting combines casting and forging, producing high-strength, lightweight parts with minimal porosity, ideal for engine blocks and suspension components. Investment casting, known for precision and intricate designs, creates complex shapes with smooth surfaces, often used for turbine blades and transmission parts. Both methods cater to the automotive industry's demand for durable, high-performance, and cost-effective components, enhancing vehicle efficiency and performance.</p></p>
