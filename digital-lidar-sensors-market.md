<p><p>Digital LiDAR sensors are categorized based on their operational range: short distance, mid distance, and long distance. Short-distance sensors, typically used for detailed topographical mapping and industrial applications, function effectively within a few hundred meters. Mid-distance sensors serve purposes like environmental monitoring and urban planning, operating within ranges of several hundred meters to a few kilometers. Long-distance sensors are designed for extensive surveying and forestry applications, capable of measuring distances up to several kilometers, providing broad spatial coverage and detailed data collection capabilities.</p></p>
