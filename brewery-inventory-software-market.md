<p><p>Brewery inventory software is classified into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based solutions are hosted on remote servers, allowing breweries to access and manage their inventory from anywhere with an internet connection. This model typically offers scalability and lower upfront costs. In contrast, on-premises software is installed directly on local servers within the brewery, providing complete control over the system and data but often requiring higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance. Both types cater to different operational needs.</p></p>
