<p><p>The epoxy reactive diluents market is categorized mainly into three types: monofunctional, bifunctional, and other types. Monofunctional diluents contain one reactive group, enhancing flow and reducing viscosity without significant crosslinking, making them suitable for coatings and adhesives. Bifunctional diluents have two reactive groups, promoting enhanced network formation and mechanical properties, ideal for applications requiring higher durability. Other types may include multifunctional or specialty diluents that offer unique performance features for specific applications in industries like automotive, aerospace, and construction.</p></p>
