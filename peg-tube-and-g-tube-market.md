<p><p>PEG (Percutaneous Endoscopic Gastrostomy) tubes and G-tubes (Gastrostomy tubes) are medical devices used for feeding patients who are unable to consume food orally. PEG tubes are primarily inserted through the abdominal wall into the stomach using an endoscopic procedure, while G-tubes are surgically placed directly into the stomach. Both devices facilitate nutritional support, hydration, and medication delivery in patients with conditions that impair swallowing, such as neurological disorders or cancers.</p><p>The PEG Tube and G-tube Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 15% during the forecast period, driven by factors such as the increasing prevalence of chronic diseases, the aging population, and advancements in medical technology. There is a growing emphasis on minimally invasive procedures, which enhances patient recovery and reduces hospital stays, further propelling market growth. Recent trends also indicate a rise in home healthcare, leading to a demand for these feeding tubes for patients who prefer receiving care at home. Additionally, innovations in tube design and materials are improving comfort and reducing complications, contributing to a more favorable market outlook in this segment.</p></p>
