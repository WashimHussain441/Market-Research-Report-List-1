<p><p>The PVC Paste by Emulsion Method market is primarily divided into two types: Homopolymeric PVC Paste and Co-polymeric PVC Paste. Homopolymeric PVC Paste is produced from a single type of vinyl chloride monomer, offering high clarity and excellent chemical resistance, making it suitable for various applications, including coatings and adhesives. In contrast, Co-polymeric PVC Paste blends different monomers, enhancing flexibility, toughness, and thermal stability, thus broadening its use in products like films, moldings, and specialized coatings.</p></p>
