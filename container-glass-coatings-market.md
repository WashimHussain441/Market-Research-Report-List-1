<p><p>Container glass coatings are categorized into water-based and oil-based types, each serving distinct purposes in the glass packaging industry. Water-based coatings are environmentally friendly, offering easy application and quick drying times, making them suitable for food and beverage containers. In contrast, oil-based coatings provide excellent durability, chemical resistance, and a glossy finish, ideal for decorative bottles and jars. The choice between these types depends on the desired performance, environmental considerations, and specific application requirements in the market.</p></p>
