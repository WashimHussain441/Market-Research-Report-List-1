<p><p>The Aircraft Specialty Fasteners Market is divided into two main types: threaded and non-threaded fasteners. Threaded fasteners, such as bolts and screws, provide adjustable and secure connections essential for aircraft assembly and repair. They enable easy disassembly and reassembly, vital for maintenance. Non-threaded fasteners, including rivets and clamps, offer permanent or semi-permanent joins, ensuring structural integrity under various stresses. Both types play critical roles in enhancing safety, durability, and performance in aviation applications.</p></p>
