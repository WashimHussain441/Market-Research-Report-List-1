<p><strong>The Automotive Infrared Night Vision System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Infrared Night Vision System</li><li>Passive Infrared Night Vision System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Infrared Night Vision System market comprises two main types: Active and Passive Infrared Night Vision Systems. Active Infrared Systems use infrared light emitted by onboard sources, illuminating the environment for enhanced visibility in low-light conditions. In contrast, Passive Infrared Systems detect naturally occurring infrared radiation emitted by objects, such as pedestrians or animals, reflecting their thermal signatures. Both technologies aim to improve nighttime driving safety by providing clearer images and enhancing the driver's perception of the surroundings.</p></p>
