<p><p>The lead-acid battery market comprises several types, including VRLA (Valve Regulated Lead Acid) batteries, flooded batteries, and others. VRLA batteries are sealed, maintenance-free, and often used in applications like backup power and renewable energy systems. Flooded batteries have liquid electrolyte, require regular maintenance, and are commonly used in industrial applications and vehicles. Other types include gel batteries and AGM (Absorbent Glass Mat) batteries, which cater to various needs such as deep cycling and high-performance applications.</p></p>
