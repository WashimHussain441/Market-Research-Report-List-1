<p><p>The e-nose market encompasses various technologies for odor detection and analysis. Conducting polymers detect gases through changes in electrical conductivity, offering flexibility and sensitivity. Metal-oxide sensors operate by altering resistance in response to gas exposure, providing cost-effective detection. Other emerging technologies include biological sensors and electronic systems, expanding applications in food safety, environmental monitoring, and healthcare.</p></p>
