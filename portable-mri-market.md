<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Portable MRI Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Superconducting-magnet-based Portable MRI</li><li>Electromagnet-based Portable MRI</li><li>Permanent-magnet-based Portable MRI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable MRI market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Superconducting-magnet-based Portable MRI** utilizes superconducting materials to produce strong magnetic fields, ensuring high-quality imaging but often requiring complex cooling systems. </p><p>2. **Electromagnet-based Portable MRI** employs electromagnets, offering flexibility in design and operation, while generally being less powerful than superconducting systems. </p><p>3. **Permanent-magnet-based Portable MRI** uses fixed magnets, resulting in a compact and energy-efficient device, though with limitations in imaging strength compared to superconducting options. Each type serves specific applications in diagnostic imaging.</p></p>
