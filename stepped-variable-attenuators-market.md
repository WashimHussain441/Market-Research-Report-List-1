<p><p>The stepped variable attenuators market comprises two main types: manually variable attenuators and continuously variable attenuators. Manually variable attenuators allow users to adjust signal levels in discrete steps, offering precise control but requiring physical adjustments. In contrast, continuously variable attenuators provide a smooth range of attenuation without fixed increments, allowing for finer signal tuning. Both types are essential in various applications, including telecommunications and audio systems, where precise signal management is crucial for optimal performance.</p></p>
