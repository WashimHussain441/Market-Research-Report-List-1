<p><p>Radiation detection, monitoring, and safety market encompass various technologies that measure ionizing radiation. Geiger counters are widely used for their simplicity and effectiveness in detecting alpha, beta, and gamma radiation. Scintillation detectors use crystals that emit light when exposed to radiation, providing high sensitivity. Solid-state detectors rely on semiconductor materials for direct measurement, offering compactness and precision. Other types include ionization chambers and thermal neutron detectors, catering to specific applications in environmental monitoring, medical diagnostics, and industrial processes.</p></p>
