<p><p>The medical surgical lasers market comprises various types, each with distinct applications. CO2 lasers are primarily used for soft tissue surgeries due to their precision and minimal thermal damage. Argon lasers excel in retinal procedures and dermatology, emitting visible light for effective tissue targeting. Nd:YAG lasers are versatile, utilized in both surgical and aesthetic treatments, offering deeper tissue penetration. Diode lasers are compact and energy-efficient, often used in dental and dermatological applications. Other lasers may include Er:YAG and excimer lasers, serving specialized medical needs.</p></p>
