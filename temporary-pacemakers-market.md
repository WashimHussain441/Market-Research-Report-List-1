<p><p>The temporary pacemaker market encompasses different types based on chamber configurations. A single-chamber temporary pacemaker stimulates one cardiac chamber, often used for basic pacing needs. The dual-chamber temporary pacemaker interacts with both the atrium and ventricle, providing more coordinated cardiac function. A triple-chamber temporary pacemaker, less common, addresses conduction issues in all three chambers, enhancing synchronization in complex cases. Each type serves specific clinical scenarios, reflecting varied patient needs and cardiac conditions.</p></p>
