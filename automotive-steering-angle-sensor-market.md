<p><p>The automotive steering angle sensor market is primarily divided into two types: digital and analog sensors. Digital sensors provide precise measurements by converting analog signals into digital data, allowing for advanced features like enhanced vehicle dynamics and stability control. In contrast, analog sensors deliver continuous voltage signals that vary with the steering position, offering simplicity and reliability. Both types play crucial roles in modern vehicles, enhancing safety and performance by enabling accurate monitoring of the steering input.</p></p>
