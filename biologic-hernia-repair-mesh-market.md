<p><p>The biologic hernia repair mesh market includes two main types: partially absorbable and fully absorbable hernia repair meshes. Partially absorbable meshes combine synthetic and biological materials, providing temporary support while allowing tissue integration, ultimately reducing long-term foreign body presence. Fully absorbable meshes are designed to be completely absorbed by the body over time, promoting natural healing without leaving permanent material behind. Both types aim to enhance patient outcomes and minimize complications associated with traditional synthetic meshes.</p></p>
