<p><p>An Electronic Control Unit (ECU) is an embedded system used in vehicles to control and manage various electrical systems and subsystems, such as engine performance, transmission, braking, and infotainment. ECUs are critical components in modern vehicles, enabling advanced functionalities like autonomous driving, fuel efficiency optimization, and enhanced safety features. They process data from sensors and execute commands to ensure optimal vehicle performance.</p><p>The Electronic Control Unit (ECU) Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS), electric vehicles (EVs), and connected car technologies. The **Steering Control Module (SCM)** enhances steering precision and driver assistance. The **Climate Control Module (CCM)** maintains cabin temperature and air quality, improving passenger comfort. Each module plays a critical role in modern vehicle functionality and safety.</p></p>
