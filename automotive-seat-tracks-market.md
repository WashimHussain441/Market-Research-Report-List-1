<p><ul><li>Active Locking Tracks</li><li>Passive Non-Locking Tracks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive seat tracks market is divided into two primary types: active locking tracks and passive non-locking tracks. Active locking tracks feature mechanisms that secure the seat position when engaged, allowing for adjustments while ensuring safety during driving. In contrast, passive non-locking tracks allow seat adjustments without a locking mechanism, which can enhance ease of use but might compromise stability. The choice between these types depends on factors like safety requirements, user convenience, and design preferences in automotive applications.</p></p>
