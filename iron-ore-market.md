<p><strong>The Iron Ore Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sinter feed</li><li>Pellet feed</li><li>Lump</li><li>Pellet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The iron ore market consists of several types: </p><p>1. **Sinter Feed**: Fine iron ore used in sintering, a process that agglomerates ores for efficient blast furnace use.</p><p>   </p><p>2. **Pellet Feed**: Higher-grade fines processed into pellets, enhancing furnace performance and reducing emissions. </p><p>3. **Lump**: Coarse, naturally occurring ore suitable for direct use in blast furnaces, offering high iron content.</p><p>4. **Pellets**: Spherical agglomerates created from iron ore fines, providing better reduction characteristics and lower impurities in steel production.</p></p>
