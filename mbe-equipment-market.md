<p><ul><li>Ordinary Molecular Beam Epitaxy System</li><li>Laser Molecular Beam Epitaxy System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Molecular Beam Epitaxy (MBE) equipment market comprises two main types: Ordinary Molecular Beam Epitaxy Systems and Laser Molecular Beam Epitaxy Systems. Ordinary MBE systems utilize thermal sources to deposit thin films of materials, allowing precise control over layer thickness and composition. In contrast, Laser MBE systems employ laser ablation for enhanced material deposition, offering greater precision and efficiency. Both types are crucial for fabricating semiconductor devices and advanced materials, contributing to advancements in electronics, photonics, and nanotechnology.</p></p>
