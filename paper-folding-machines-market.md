<p><p>The paper folding machines market includes various types tailored for specific folding techniques. Half-fold machines create a single crease down the center, ideal for brochures and flyers. Z-fold machines produce an accordion-style fold, commonly used for greeting cards and mailers. Double-parallel folding involves two parallel folds, suitable for detailed documents. Letter folds, or tri-folds, divide a sheet into three sections, perfect for pamphlets and informational brochures. Each type caters to distinct needs in printing and presentation.</p></p>
