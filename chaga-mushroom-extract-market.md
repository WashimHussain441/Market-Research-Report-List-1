<p><p>The Chaga mushroom extract market is primarily categorized into two types: water extract and dual extract. In contrast, dual extract employs both water and alcohol solvents, capturing a broader range of bioactive compounds, including triterpenes and antioxidants, enhancing its medicinal properties. Both extraction methods cater to varying consumer preferences and health benefits.</p></p>
